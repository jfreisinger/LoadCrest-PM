## Elevator Pitch

Next-generation AI clusters have fundamentally broken legacy AC-powered data center architecture. Nvidia and OCP are driving racks to 800VDC, but developers face massive integration roadblocks — current DC systems are retrofitted EV components that cannot handle the violent, microsecond step-loads of AI workloads, forcing them to build expensive, customized "Frankenstein" systems.

LoadCrest provides purpose-built, intelligent "connective tissue" that makes 800VDC AI factories viable, safe, and easily deployable. Our SentinelT algorithm, embedded in a modular containerized hardware platform, delivers clean 800VDC power while presenting a stable, grid-compliant load profile — saving developers up to 80% of the CapEx they would otherwise spend on buffering and filtering infrastructure.

*"They were built for a world of 20kW racks. You're deploying 200kW–1MW racks. LoadCrest was built for this."*

### vs. Retrofitted EV DC Systems
*"EV components handle smooth, predictable loads. AI workloads swing 30–100% in milliseconds. SentinelT was designed for exactly this problem."*

### vs. Custom In-House Engineering
*"Why spend 18 months and $50M engineering a custom power solution when LoadCrest ships a pre-certified, containerized system in weeks?"*

## Messaging Don'ts

- Don't lead with "800VDC" to a non-technical buyer — lead with cost savings and speed
- Don't describe SentinelT as "software" to a power engineer — it's embedded firmware with real-time control logic
- Don't use "microgrid" language with hyperscaler buyers — use "AI factory power delivery" and "grid interconnection"
- Don't undersell the Sandia/USAF validation — it is the proof point that separates LoadCrest from paper products
