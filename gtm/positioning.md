# Positioning

## Category

AI Data Center Power Infrastructure — purpose-built 800VDC power delivery and stabilization for AI factories, delivered as Energy-as-a-Service (EaaS).

## Target Customer

**Primary:** Neocloud providers and AI factory developers building 800VDC data centers — especially those deploying off-grid or pre-grid, where speed-to-power and grid interconnection complexity are acute pain points.

**Secondary:** Hyperscalers and colocation providers transitioning existing facilities to 800VDC architecture per the OCP Diablo specification.

**Economic buyer:** VP/Director of Infrastructure or CTO at an AI Factory developer; EPC procurement lead on 100MW+ projects.

**Technical champion:** Data Center Electrical Engineer or MEP consultant responsible for power architecture design.

## Key Differentiator

LoadCrest is the only purpose-built, intelligent power delivery system for 800VDC AI factories. The **Sentinel** sub-250ms control algorithm — validated in real-world 800VDC deployments with Sandia National Labs and the U.S. Air Force — is embedded in a turnkey, containerized hardware platform that sits just outside the data hall.

Competitors either sell hardware without intelligent control (legacy power electronics vendors) or force customers to build expensive, customized "Frankenstein" systems by adapting EV-grade DC components. LoadCrest delivers a complete, deployable system with an EaaS business model that aligns LoadCrest's incentives with customer uptime and efficiency.

## The Problem We Solve

- Traditional AC architecture results in **>30% power loss** through transmission, conversion, and heat
- A 100MW data center requires **~$50M of filtering & stabilizing power electronics** to deliver clean power under current approaches
- Current DC systems are **retrofitted EV components** that cannot handle the violent, microsecond step-loads of AI/GPU workloads — forcing developers to build costly custom solutions
- GPU loads swing **30–100% in milliseconds**, causing dirty power that damages compute and triggers grid interconnection denial
- Utility interconnection studies can be **delayed 2+ years** by the harmonic distortion and load volatility of large AI deployments

## Value Proposition Summary

LoadCrest saves a **100MW AI data center ~$40M in year one** through CapEx reduction on buffering and filtering infrastructure, with ongoing OpEx savings of $7M/year and efficiency gains of $3M/year. Total TCO reduction: from ~$350M to ~$129M per 100MW building block — an **80% reduction in buffering CapEx**.

Off-grid and pre-grid AI data center deployments offer the fastest path to market — these customers face acute power delivery challenges that cannot be solved with legacy approaches and have the highest urgency to deploy.
